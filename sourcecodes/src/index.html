Weather is a key variable worthy of our keenest consideration when planning schedules.

TIMELY forecast of temperature, rainfall and indeed all weather elements can aid in initiating selective interventions and decision making.

Information on weather may give insight for an early management of disease outbreaks, informing on suitable dress code, planning farming schedules and guiding itineraries amongst other unlimited number of applications.

The significance of such information is further enhanced when the location is specific and known.

It is from the foregoing facts that this project is attempted.

It is a web platform developed to map possible weather forecasts of direct consequential nature to human engagements and the relevant precautionary or advisory information regarding the circumstances.

Procedure for usage...

  • Choose a town or city from the selection list or
  • Interact with the map:
    • Pan through a desired area of interest in the map
    • Click the map in a place for which you desire information
  • Specify the nature of the forecast that suits your needs
  • Unless an alert error is given please wait for results.

NOTE: There are extra functionalities tailored for Johannesburg, South Africa.
